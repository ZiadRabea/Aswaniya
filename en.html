About Aswania

Authentic products from the heart of Aswan

Aswania… a story born in the heart of Aswan 🌴

Women decided to turn their homes into a source of income and create natural products by hand, full of love and passion 💛
Every product carries the hands of a strong woman and a dream that grows every day 👑✨

Vision / Mission / Goals

Vision

To become a leading brand for natural and heritage products from Upper Egypt, and to bring women’s products to every home in Egypt and beyond.

Mission

To empower women economically by producing and marketing high-quality products while preserving local heritage and turning it into real opportunities for work.

Goals

  1. Support women in creating and growing their own home-based projects.
  2. Offer natural products with high quality and fair prices.
  3. Build a strong brand that combines quality, authenticity, and social impact.
